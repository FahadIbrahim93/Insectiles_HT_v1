# Pinik Pipra: Visual & Feedback Strategy (2026 Studio Standard)

## 1. The "Juice" Philosophy (Layered Feedback)
Every "Hit" must feel like a micro-reward.
*   **Layer 1 (Sprite):** 1.2x scale pop + 50ms white flash + squash/stretch.
*   **Layer 2 (World):** 10px radial ripple (chromatic aberration) + 5ms screen shake (3px intensity).
*   **Layer 3 (UI):** Score popup with "Elastic" easing + Fever bar "pulse" update.
*   **Layer 4 (Audio):** 523.25Hz -> 1046.5Hz pitch escalation for combos.

## 2. Fever Mode: "The Trip"
When Fever activates:
*   **Background:** Shift from static/slow mandala to high-intensity plasma shader (Fuchsia/Cyan).
*   **Camera:** Slow "Fisheye" distortion (1.1x zoom at center).
*   **Queen Ant:** High-frame-rate "Dance" or "Crowned" idle animation.
*   **Particles:** Trail particles increase by 300%; color-cycle through neon palette.

## 3. UI Overhaul (Based on Stitch Design)
*   **Glassmorphism HUD:** Translucent dark panels with neon borders.
*   **Odometer Score:** Use a monospace font with a sliding vertical animation for digit changes.
*   **Mandala Lanes:** Subtle, rotating mandala glows at the strike zone (bottom 150px).

## 4. Viral Share Moments
*   **Combo Fireworks:** At x10 combo, trigger a screen-wide "kaleidoscope" particle burst.
*   **Result Card:** Generate a high-contrast PNG of the end-score with the "New Best!" badge for easy social sharing.
